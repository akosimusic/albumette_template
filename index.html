Franco Emil is a music product, composer, and guitarist whose wide musical taste ranges from the classical and folk to the eclectic sounds of jazz, pop, and electronica. His influences developed from his artistry for film score, sound design, and exploration of collaborations among artists with varied backgrounds. Apart from this, his is also the lead guitarist and one of the composers and arrangers of the band Why July. He debuted his first instrumental, lo-fi album “My Side” in 2021 and his latest solo release is an EP entitled “Here I Am”.
